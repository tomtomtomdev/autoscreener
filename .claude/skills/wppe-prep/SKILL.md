# WPPE Preparation (Wakil Perantara Pedagang Efek)

A mentor's roadmap for the **WPPE — Wakil Perantara Pedagang Efek** (Broker-Dealer Representative), the Indonesian license required to act as a representative of a securities brokerage (perantara pedagang efek). This is the local *regulatory* license for the **brokerage/securities-dealing and sales** path — distinct from WMI, which is for fund management (see `wmi-prep`), and WPEE, which is for underwriting.

## Verify before relying on specifics

Licensing is set by **OJK (Otoritas Jasa Keuangan)**; professional certification exams run through the **Panitia Standar Profesi** framework and are prepared/administered by **TICMI (The Indonesia Capital Market Institute)**, part of the IDX group. The WPPE framework has been **restructured into tiers**, exam modules and fees change, and continuing-education (PPL) rules apply. Treat the structure below as a map — **confirm the current tier structure and requirements with TICMI and OJK** before committing.

## What it is and who it's for

WPPE is for people working in **securities brokerage** — dealing, executing, and selling securities on behalf of clients at a sekuritas firm. If the goal is managing portfolios/funds rather than brokering trades, the relevant license is WMI; for underwriting securities issues, it is WPEE.

## The tiered structure (verify current form)

The brokerage license has been split into levels of scope, commonly described as:

- **WPPE-Pemasaran Terbatas** — limited marketing scope (the entry tier, narrowest activities).
- **WPPE-Pemasaran** — marketing scope (selling/distributing securities to clients).
- **WPPE (full)** — the complete broker-dealer representative scope, including the dealing functions.

The lighter tiers have fewer/shorter exam modules and suit client-facing sales roles; the full WPPE covers the broadest activities. Confirm the exact current tiers, their permitted activities, and module counts with TICMI.

## The path (certification then license)

1. **Sit the WPPE certification exam(s)** for the relevant tier through TICMI / the Panitia Standar Profesi.
2. **Obtain the certification**, then **apply to OJK for the individual license** (izin orang perseorangan).
3. **Maintain the license** via any required continuing professional education and renewal.

## Exam subject areas (typical — verify current syllabus)

WPPE content centers on the mechanics and rules of brokerage:

- **Capital-market law and regulation** — the Undang-Undang Pasar Modal and OJK rules governing brokers and trading.
- **Code of ethics and professional conduct** — client duties, suitability, conflicts of interest.
- **Securities trading mechanics** — how the IDX market operates: order types, lot size, tick sizes, the ARA/ARB auto-rejection limits, settlement.
- **Securities products** — equities, bonds, and other instruments and their characteristics.
- **Know-Your-Customer / AML** — client onboarding, suitability, and anti-money-laundering obligations.

The regulation, market-mechanics, and KYC/AML content is the jurisdiction-specific core and the heart of the exam.

## How to prepare (sequencing)

1. **Pick the right tier first** — don't over-license; match the tier to the role (a sales role may only need a marketing tier).
2. **Take the TICMI preparation course** for that tier — it maps to the current modules.
3. **Front-load regulation, market mechanics, and KYC/AML** — these are the most tested and most Indonesia-specific.
4. **Practice per-module questions** and confirm you can clear each threshold before sitting.

## How this connects to the broader study

WPPE is lighter on valuation than WMI or the CFA — its center of gravity is **rules, market mechanics, and client conduct** rather than security analysis. The IDX market-mechanics knowledge (lot size, tick sizes, ARA/ARB, settlement) is exactly the microstructure that also makes paper trading and live trading realistic, so it complements the `investing-curriculum` skill's practice phases. Someone analytical who also wants to work in brokerage would pair the curriculum (analysis) with WPPE (the license and market-mechanics rigor).
